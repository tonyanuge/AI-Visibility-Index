What the AI Visibility Index does

Visibility

Are you recommended?

For the questions customers actually ask, we record whether a business is named by the AI at all — or absent from the answer entirely.

Rank & share

Where do you stand?

When a business is named, the Index scores its position and share of recommendations against others in the same market.

Accuracy

Does AI get you right?

We note where the assistants state something about a business — and flag where captured answers got facts wrong, by review.

How it works

  1. We pose the real buying-intent questions for a market to AI assistants under stated, dated conditions.
  2. The captured answers are reviewed and scored for visibility, rank, share and accuracy.
  3. You enter your business name in the free checker and get an instant verdict from the scored data.
  4. If you're invisible or behind, you can request the full report for your market.

Honest boundaries

We only claim what the build supports.

  • Dated snapshot, not a guarantee. Every result reflects what AI assistants returned under stated conditions on a given date. Positions drift, and answers vary by location and session. We make no guaranteed-ranking claim and cannot promise placement in any AI answer.
  • Demo data is synthetic. Until real captures are imported for a market, any sample figures are clearly-labelled synthetic demo data and are not a publishable Index.
  • Monitoring is opt-in. Automated live answer-fetching and ongoing monitoring run only when explicitly configured; otherwise captures are collected manually. We don't claim always-on, fully-automated AI monitoring unless it is set up.
  • Independent. Not affiliated with OpenAI, Google or Perplexity.
